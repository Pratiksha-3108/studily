About the Company

Studily XR is a pioneering immersive technology initiative by Studily Learning Technologies, committed to transforming education and industry through Virtual Reality (VR) innovation.

We empower students and professionals to explore, interact, and understand complex concepts through lifelike virtual environments — delivering measurable, scalable, and safe hands-on learning.

Our Mission & Vision

Shaping the future of immersive learning with purpose and innovation.

Our Mission

To make immersive learning accessible, scalable, and impactful by empowering every student and professional with world-class AR/VR/MR experiences across India and beyond.

Our Vision

To become a global leader in immersive education and training by transforming how people learn, practice, and master skills through future-ready virtual simulation technologies.

Message from the Founder

"Experience is the most powerful teacher. Our goal is to democratize hands-on learning — anywhere, safely."

At StudilyXR, we combine pedagogy, realistic simulation and scalable technology to make skill-building accessible and measurable. We partner with institutions and enterprises to deliver tailored learning journeys that drive real outcomes.

— Kaivalya Nagpure, Founder & CEO
